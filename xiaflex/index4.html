WHAT IS DUPUYTREN’S CONTRACTURE?

Treatment with XIAFLEX®

XIAFLEX® is an FDA-approved, nonsurgical treatment for adults with Dupuytren’s contracture (DC) when a cord can be felt. It starts working as soon as you receive the first injection.

XIAFLEX® is given:

  • Collagen builds up in the hand beneath the surface of the skin
  • By a hand specialist with training specifically for XIAFLEX®

Risk factors of DC

Some people are more likely to have DC. Below are a few of the risk factors.

Age

People over age 40

Family history of DC

Often runs in families

Smoking

May increase the risk of DC

Gender

Men are more likely to have DC

Family background

People with ancestors from Northern Europe

Diabetes

Also believed to increase the risk of DC
